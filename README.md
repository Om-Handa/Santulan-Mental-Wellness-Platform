SANTULAN- A digital mental wellness platform. Developed specially for College Students of India to reduce their Stigma. It is an prototype for my platform which was an idea for Smart India Hackathon. Santulan Has following features:

1. AI-Based Screening: AI-based questions and detection of Mental issues.
2. Personalized suggestions: Depending on the AI-test scores students get perosnalized prescription
3. Gamified Platform: Tasks, resoureces, achievements etc in a gamified way.
4. Counsellor Connectiom: Direct connection to college counsellor with full privacy.
5. Peer Support Forum: End to end encrypted chatting platform.
6. Admin Dashboard: Dashboard for admins(counsellors) to check growth, and handle all students.
